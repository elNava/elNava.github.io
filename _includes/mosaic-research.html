Metals 2023, ESO

I attended the second edition "Metal Production and Distribution in a Hierarchical Universe – II" organized by ESO at Vitacura, Chile. I gave a talk about the work I am doing regarding the gas-phase metallicity of local analogs of high-z galaxies and show some of the results.

Sep 4-9th, 2023
IMPRS-HD Summer School 2023

I attended the summer school of the International Max-Planck Research School on "Unraveling Galaxy Evolution with the JWST" in its version of 2023. We learnt about all the details of this new and revolutionary space telescope, the capabilities of its instruments, and how to prepare our own observations, proposals and data reduction. Also, we had world class lectures on how JWST can be used to probe from the small scale details in nearby galaxies to the very high redshift Universe.

Jun 12-13th, 2023
Observation Run at Las Campanas Observatory

We won two night of observation in Las Campanas Observatory. It was my first time flying and being in a world class observatory. I learned a lot about the observations, how everything works at the top of the mountains, and I had the opportunity to gather optical an near-IR spectra of more galaxies that will be part of my masters' thesis. One of the best experiences I have had in astronomy so far.

Feb 15th - May 6th, 2023
ESO Internship

I did a 2.5 month science internship at ESO in Vitacura, Chile. With my supervisor there, Belén Alcalde-Pampliega, we studied the morphology and physical properties of galaxies at z ~ 1 to z ~ 10, using deep imaging of JWST/NIRcam and complement archival data from past surveys (HST, Spitzer, ALMA, etc).
