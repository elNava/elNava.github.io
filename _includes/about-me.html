About me

I am a master's student in Astronomy at the Department of Astronomy (DAS) of Universidad de Chile. I do research in galaxy evolution, but I am particularly interested in the physics of galaxies in the first billion-years from the Big-Bang.

I am also a huge enthusiastic outside academy. I like teaching and I have been involved in several public activities on outreach. We have a tremendous amount of knowledge waiting to be shared with people. Non communicated science is not science!

I love to do a lot of things, from sharing with friends, to some sports, and even board games like chess. But what I like above almost everything is to spend time with my three little cats.
